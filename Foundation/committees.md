# The Applied Machine Learning Collective of the Rockies Committees List and Mission

AMLC of the Rockies operates through volunteer committees that drive specific aspects of the community. Each committee has a charter that defines its mission, scope, and responsibilities.

## Marketing/Outreach
### Mission
The Marketing/Outreach Committee is responsible for promoting AMLC events, managing social media presence, creating content, and building partnerships with organizations and sponsors.

### Charter
[View Charter](/.github/Committees/outreach-marketing.md)

## Organizers
### Mission
The Organizers Committee handles event planning, logistics, venue coordination, and ensures smooth execution of all AMLC activities and programs.

### Charter
[View Charter](/.github/Committees/organizers.md)

## How to Join a Committee

Interested in contributing? Committee membership is open to AMLC members. To join:

1. Review the committee charter
2. Attend a committee meeting (schedules in Discord)
3. Reach out to committee leads or email leadership@amlcoftherockies.org

## Proposing a New Committee

If you see a need for a new committee, submit a [Community Enhancement Proposal](/Foundation/proposals.md) outlining:
- The committee's purpose and mission
- Proposed scope and responsibilities
- Initial members and leadership
- How it aligns with AMLC's mission
